Valuation Methodology

This is an ASSET-BASED valuation, not revenue-based. Current revenue (~$12K/year) reflects limited production due to funding constraints—NOT the value of assets built. $500K+ already invested in startup costs, 89 original 3D productions, brand development, and studio infrastructure. The valuation combines: (1) Subscriber Base Value ($0.50-$1.00 per subscriber × 739K = $370K-$739K, COPPA-adjusted for Made-for-Kids content), (2) Content Library (89 original 3D productions at ~$8,200/video = $730K), (3) Production Company (SS Media Productions LLC with B2B client relationships), and (4) Founder/Leadership retention. The final range of $1.8M-$2.5M reflects COPPA restrictions on kids content (no notification bell, no comments, no personalized ads). Acquirer gets proven foundation ready to scale. Note: Ongoing production (~$70-85K/year) required for content continuity and brand relevance.
