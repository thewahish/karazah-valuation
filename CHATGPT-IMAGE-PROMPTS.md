## Character Reference Notes

For consistent Karazah character representation:

### Tootah (Girl Character)
- Pink/red twin ponytails
- Big expressive eyes
- Red or pink outfit
- Cheerful, friendly expression
- Age appearance: 5-7 years

### Mishmish (Boy Character)
- Brown hair, slightly messy
- Friendly round face
- Blue or green outfit
- Playful, curious expression
- Age appearance: 5-7 years

### Style Guidelines
- Pixar/Disney-quality 3D rendering
- Bright, saturated colors
- Child-friendly, non-threatening expressions
- Educational/wholesome atmosphere
- Arabic cultural elements when appropriate
