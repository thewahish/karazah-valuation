## Valuation Methodology

### 1. Revenue Analysis
- Current monetization streams (AdSense, sponsorships)
- Historical revenue trends
- Growth projections with multiple scenarios

### 2. Audience Analytics
- Subscriber growth patterns (749K and growing)
- Engagement metrics and watch time
- Geographic distribution (MENA focus)
- Demographic composition

### 3. Content Portfolio Assessment
- 1,135+ video library value
- Top-performing content categories
- Intellectual property valuation

### 4. Market Benchmarking
- Comparable Arabic children's channels
- Industry-standard valuation multiples
- Regional market positioning

### 5. Strategic Value Assessment
- Brand recognition and equity
- Spacetoon partnership synergy potential
- Cross-platform expansion opportunities

**Primary:** Establish professional valuation for Spacetoon partnership leverage
